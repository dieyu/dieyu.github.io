A good shot is "right write" and "wrong run".

The important part is to establish "wrong run".

Sometime ago, I made a test, using audio files of "wrong" and "run", from online dictionaries(Cambridge, Collins, and Oxford), with minor changes in pitch and tempo.

While these audio files are absolutely clear(more clear than normal conversation), second language learners usually get confused, and native speakers rarely get confused(error happens).

You can work on this.
